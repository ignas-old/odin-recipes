Cooked Salmon w/ broccoli

Simple salted salmon with boiled broccoli. Tasty and healthy for fans of fish!

Ingredients:

  • Salted Salmon Fillet
  • Broccoli
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Recipe:

  1. Pour some olive oil on the pan and heat it to mid high level.
  2. Wash the salmon with water and dry it with paper towel.
  3. Cook each side of the Salmon Fillet with seasoning of your choosing for about 5mins
  4. Boil the broccoli for about 5mins after dissolving the butter in the water.
  5. Serve the salmon and broccoli and enjoy the meal!
